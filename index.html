How it works

Three steps to chaos

🃏

Step 01

Make your friends into cards

Give each friend stats, a special move, and a quip. The more accurate, the more devastating.

🗂

Step 02

Build your deck

Pick 6 cards, choose your holo, name your deck. Type matchups and synergies matter.

⚔️

Step 03

Battle

Best of 3 rounds. Pick your attack, use gear cards, trigger holo abilities.
